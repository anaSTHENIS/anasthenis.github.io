Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the D&D Event Announcer Bot collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information We Collect

The bot may collect the following types of information:

  • Server Information: Server IDs, channel IDs, and role information necessary for bot functionality.
  • User Information: User IDs and usernames for event announcements and bot interactions.
  • Event Data: Event details you provide, including titles, descriptions, dates, and times.
  • Usage Data: Command usage and interaction logs for improving bot performance.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide core bot functionality, including event announcements and reminders.
  • Improve and optimize bot performance and user experience.
  • Troubleshoot issues and provide technical support.
  • Ensure compliance with Discord's Terms of Service and Community Guidelines.

Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain event data and user information only as long as necessary to provide bot services. You may request deletion of your data by contacting our support team.

Third-Party Services

The bot operates on Discord's platform and is subject to Discord's Privacy Policy. We do not share your information with third parties except as required for bot functionality or legal compliance.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we have collected about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Opt out of certain data collection practices.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through appropriate channels.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact our support team.
